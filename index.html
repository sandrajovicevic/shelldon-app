      • Pick a mode: tasks to do, or everyday life stuff.

      • Miss one? It'll be waiting for you next time you open the app — no guilt, but no hiding either.
      • Streak counts days you actually follow through.
      • No account needed. Use the 💾 backup button to move your streak and history to a new phone.
